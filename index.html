Our Story

JK Soda Water was founded in 1994 with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality cold drinks to families across the region. What started as a small local operation has grown into a trusted brand known for consistency, quality, and taste.

For over 30 years, we have maintained our commitment to excellence. We believe that quality is not just about the product, but about the experience we provide to every customer. Our secret lies in using only the finest ingredients, maintaining strict quality control, and listening to our customers' needs.

Every bottle of JK Soda Water is a testament to our dedication. We're proud to be part of your family celebrations, your refreshing summers, and your special moments. Thank you for making us your preferred choice!
